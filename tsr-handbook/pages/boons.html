Chapter IV

Starting Boons

Regardless of race or origin, every character picks one Boon. These defining choices shape your entire career.

Pick exactly 1 Boon during character creation. Some Boons carry permanent restrictions — read carefully before choosing.
History of Discipline
You have devoted your life to the tireless pursuit of mastery, and that's never been enough.

Gain: +2 Skill Points and +10 Attribute Points.
At 4th level: obtain +1 additional Skill Point.
History of Knowledge
You have devoted your life to study and the pursuit of understanding all the world's mysteries.

Gain: −2 to Resource Cost on all abilities. When successfully damaging an enemy with an Attack, inflict Fumble upon them.
Blessing of Power
You have obtained power beyond mortal comprehension due to the favor of a higher power.

Gain: +1 to Primary Damage, Spell Power, Accuracy, and Parry.
At 4th level: these buffs increase to +2, and you also gain +2 Armor and Ward.
Vow of Ash
You have taken the Vow of Ash, bestowing physical strength beyond mortal limits — at the cost of all magical potential.

Gain: When leveling up any Physical skill (Martial Arts, Tactics, Endurance, CQC, Savagery, Marksmanship), gain an extra mastery perk upon completion.
✖ Cannot invest Skill Points into Spellcrafting or any Magic Skills. Cannot increase Mana through any means.
Rite of Embers
You have undergone the Rite of Embers, blessing you with magical power beyond mortal limits — at the cost of all physical potential.

Gain: When leveling up any Magic Skill or Spellcrafting, gain an additional mastery perk upon completion.
✖ Cannot invest Skill Points into Endurance, Martial Arts, Tactics, CQC, Savagery, or Marksmanship. Cannot increase Stamina through any means.
Gigantic
Your body is naturally larger than those around you, granting formidable physical advantage through primal strength.

Gain: Your body occupies a 2×2 space on the battlefield. Melee reach +1 space. Strength and Vigor start at d6 instead of d4, and can reach a maximum of d12+6.
Multi-Limbed
Whether by natural birth, prosthetics, or magical means, you have multiple arms — all usable simultaneously.

Gain: 4 arms instead of 2. May draw, stow, or swap up to 4 weapons per Draw Action.
Winged
Natural wings allow your character to take flight, granting freedom of movement impossible for grounded fighters.

Gain: Flight Speed of 50ft.
