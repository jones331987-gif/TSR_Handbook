Chapter III

Skills

Skill trees define what your character can do. Click any skill to expand its details.

Skill Points: Start with 3. Gain 1 per level up. Ranks: Novice (1 pt) · Adept (2 pts) · Master (3 pts). You must learn lower-rank spells/techniques in a line before learning higher ones.

⚔ Physical Skills

Close Quarters Combat (CQC)

Physical ▾

Governs melee armaments using Strength and Dexterity. Unlocks Techniques at each rank.

  • Adept: Proficiency with all CQC weapons. Can now perform Attacks of Opportunity. If AoO deals HP damage, Reaction is refunded.

Master Perks (choose 1):

Brute
Use Strength instead of Dexterity to determine Accuracy with Melee weapons.
Crush Armor
First melee attack has AP equal to half your Strength. Critical hits also apply Rend.
Lethality
Dealing HP damage with a Melee Weapon applies 2 stacks of Bleed. Aggravate on a Crit.

Marksmanship

Physical ▾

Governs ranged armaments (bows, crossbows, firearms, throwing weapons) using Strength and Dexterity.

  • Adept: Proficiency with all Marksmanship weapons.

Master Perks (choose 1):

Eagle Eye
Use Dexterity for damage with Ranged Weapons. On crit, add an extra Dex die instead of a weapon die.
Overwatch
Perform Attacks of Opportunity with Ranged Weapons at half effective range. Enhancers cost double when used this way.
Barbed Shots
Ranged HP damage applies 2 Bleed stacks. Aggravate on crit.

Endurance

Physical ▾

Hone your body to trade blows in melee and wear armor more effectively.

Master Perks (choose 1):

Endure Pain
Spend Stamina during a defensive phase to cancel damage: every 2 Stamina = 1 damage cancelled. Cap: 10 per phase.
Landshark
Use Techniques and receive Light Armor bonuses even while wearing Heavy Armor.
Punishing Draw
The first attack of your player phase always has +2 Primary Damage and +2 Accuracy.

Martial Arts

Physical ▾

Governs martial maneuvers and battlefield control techniques.

Master Perks (choose 1):

Style Master
Use 3 Attack Enhancers per phase instead of 2. The most expensive enhancer costs 2×. 2-round cooldown.
Enduring Warrior
+10 to maximum Damage Shield cap. At the start of Upkeep, automatically regain 5 Damage Shields.
Disciplined Fighter
Halve the cost of 1 Attack Enhancer each time you declare an attack phase.

Tactics

Physical ▾

The art of command and positional analysis. Buff allies, read the battlefield, and outmaneuver foes.

Master Perks (choose 1):

Tactical Genius
Cast 1 Tactics buff as a Free Action each turn, but at +2 Stamina cost.
Perfect Positioning
Gain 1 stack of Stability at the start of your Upkeep Phase, for free.
Outnumbered Positioning
When attacked, redirect the first hit toward another nearby enemy (making it Guard you). Once per defensive phase, costs 4 Stamina.

Savagery

Physical ▾

Lose yourself in primal fury, letting ancient instincts take over. Raw power over finesse.

Master Perks (choose 1):

Primal Mass
Use Vigor instead of Strength for melee attack damage.
Warrior's Ichor
When you deal HP damage in an attack phase, transfer the last negative condition applied to you onto your target (using your own modifiers for the Save).
Savage Fighting
Parry bonuses from Unarmored Fighting also add to Primary Damage.

✦ Magic Skills

Spellcrafting

Magic ▾

Manipulate arcane energy with precision. Unlocks powerful magic Techniques and foundational spell mastery.

Master Perks (choose 1):

Blessing of Mana
Each time you cast a spell, roll a Mana Blessing check (d20, 11+). On success, your next spell costs 0 Mana. 1-round cooldown after activation.
Find Familiar
Bond with a chosen summon. It no longer uses your Concentration slot, you gain a free action to command it exclusively, and its range extends to 1 mile.
Wizard
Learn and slot spells from schools you haven't invested in. Use Spellcrafting skill for scaling. +2 slot cost penalty. Training spells cannot be learned this way.

Anima Magic

Magic ▾

Elemental Anima magic: Fire, Water, Earth, Air, and Lightning. Harness the raw forces of nature.

Master Perks (choose 1):

Energy
+5 Mana. Grants the Redirect spell.
Domination
Sustained spells cost 1 less Mana per round. Unlocks Anima Touch Magic.
Magnitude — Elemental Soul
Attune to a single element for major bonuses, but lose access to all other element types. Choose from: Infernal · Aqua · Terra · Vacuum · Thunderous Soul.

Elemental Souls:

🔥 Infernal Soul
All flame hits deal +1d6 fire secondary damage. +1 Searing stack on application. Absorb/extinguish small fires.
💧 Aqua Soul
Water hits deal 1d8 Stamina damage. Control water state. Breathe underwater.
🪨 Terra Soul
Use Strength for Earth spell accuracy and damage. Cast Earth spells via Touch Magic. Grant 10 Damage Shields on Earth cast (excess goes to an ally).
🌪 Vacuum Soul
Wind damage knocks back 2 spaces. Sacrifice damage for Accuracy. Fly at base movement +2.
⚡ Thunderous Soul
Static aura: Lightning spells and Lightning-enchanted weapons gain the Fatal quality.

Void Magic

Magic ▾

Manipulation of space, distance, and arcane displacement.

Master Perks (choose 1):

Arcane Spirit
+5 Mana. Grants the Drifter's Sheathe spell.
Arcane Order
Sustained spells cost 1 less Mana per round. Unlocks Void Touch Magic.
Arcane Chaos
+2 spaces to Teleportation, Forced Movement, Range Enhancers, and effective spell range for all Void spells.

Holy Magic

Magic ▾

Divine light magic. Effective against Creatures of Darkness. Prevents regeneration in affected creatures until Short Rest.

Master Perks (choose 1):

Holy Might
+5 Mana. Grants Mitotic Regeneration spell.
Angelic Radiance
Cast Holy spells via Touch Magic. Create CQC and Marksmanship weapons with Manifest Relic / Manifest Angelic Relic spells.
Divine Mind
Spend a 3rd Action on a Holy spell to empower it — increases magnitude and can affect up to 4 targets.

Thaumaturgy

Magic ▾

Magic of artifice and constructed power. Thaumic weapons and constructs.

Master Perks (choose 1):

Thaumic Battery
+5 Mana. Grants the Replicate Magic Item spell.
Thaumic Might
Successful Thaumic weapon hits generate Damage Shields equal to the weapon's damage die. Create CQC and Marksmanship weapons via Manifest Weapons / Manifest Ethereal Weapons.
Thaumic Power
Maintain 2 self-buffs in 1 Concentration slot (Master + Adept max). Both can be cast in the same Pre-Battle action. +2 to all resource costs while active.

Figment Magic

Magic ▾

Illusion, stealth, and mind-bending deception. Manipulate perception and plant shadows in enemy minds.

Master Perks (choose 1):

Shadow Thief
+5 Mana. Grants the Shadow God's Fingers spell.
Diplomat
Cast Figment spells stealthily (enemy can't see it and can't defensive action). Costs 3 Actions.
Conqueror
All Figment debuffs become 10ft radius targeted AoEs. +1 magnitude to all Figment spells and +1 to their stack applications.

Dark Magic

Magic ▾

Elder power and shadowed sorcery. Effective against creatures of light and summons. Includes summon manipulation.

Master Perks (choose 1):

Sorcerer
+5 Mana. Grants the Empathy spell.
Hexer
Sustained spells cost 1 less Mana per round. Cast Elder spells via Touch Magic.
Necromancer
All summons gain 5 Damage Shields on summoning. Summon attacks deal +1d6 Dark secondary damage.

Nature Magic

Magic ▾

Draw power from the living world — beasts, growth, and primal earth-force.

Master Perks (choose 1):

Druidcraft
+5 Mana. Grants the Polymorph spell.
Nature's Wrath
Sustained spells cost 1 less Mana per round. Cast Nature spells via Touch Magic.
Soulbeast
Merge with a summoned Nature animal (uses Concentration slot). Gain extra stats and unique abilities from the merged animal. All attacks count as Flanking while merged.
